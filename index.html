How SmartSahyata Works

Explore Personalized Scheme Pages

Each government scheme gets its own dedicated page filled with complete details — purpose, benefits, required documents, deadlines, and eligibility criteria.

Run Instant Eligibility Check

Our AI agent guides users through a quick, intelligent screening process. It checks real-time eligibility against official parameters.

Get Tailored Access to Application Forms

Based on eligibility outcome, the system unlocks the application form for that specific scheme, with relevant fields and simplified instructions.

Submit Confidently, Track Easily

Approved applicants are guided to submit forms securely. A tracking ID helps them monitor the status of their application with full transparency.

What Makes SmartSahyata a Game-Changer?

  • Fully Customized Scheme Pages

    Every scheme has a dedicated, content-rich page that simplifies understanding and eliminates confusion.

  • Eligibility-First Form Access

    No irrelevant forms. No wasted time. Forms only appear after confirming eligibility — tailored to the citizen's unique profile.

  • Dynamic Form Generation

    Every form adapts in real time based on user input. No manual adjustments, no ambiguity.

  • Policy-Grade Precision

    Rules are mapped directly from official government guidelines — ensuring accurate approvals and rejections, with documented reasoning.
