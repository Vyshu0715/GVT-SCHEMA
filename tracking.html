Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process an application?

The processing time varies depending on the scheme. Typically, it takes 15-30 days from submission to final approval. However, complex applications or those requiring additional verification may take longer.

How will I be notified about status changes?

You will receive SMS notifications on your registered mobile number at each stage of the application process. You can also check the status anytime using the tracking system on this page.

What should I do if my application is rejected?

If your application is rejected, you'll receive a notification explaining the reason. You can address the issues mentioned and reapply, or contact our help center for assistance with the reapplication process.

Can I update my application after submission?

Once submitted, you cannot directly update your application. However, if you need to make corrections or provide additional information, please contact our help center with your application ID and details of the changes required.

What happens after my application is approved?

After approval, the benefits will be disbursed according to the scheme guidelines. This may include direct bank transfers, issuance of cards or certificates, or enrollment in training programs. You'll receive detailed instructions once your application is approved.
