Education Scholarship Scheme

Financial support for students to pursue higher education and achieve their academic goals

Overview

The Education Scholarship Scheme is designed to provide financial assistance to meritorious and economically backward students in Andhra Pradesh, enabling them to pursue higher education without financial constraints. The scheme aims to support students in covering tuition fees, hostel expenses, books, and other educational needs.

Launched by the Department of Higher Education, Government of Andhra Pradesh, in 2022, this scheme endeavors to reduce dropout rates and promote access to quality education for all. Over 100,000 students across the state have benefited from this initiative since its inception.

Key Features of the Scheme

  • Financial assistance up to ₹25,000 per academic year
  • Coverage of tuition fees and examination fees
  • Support for hostel accommodation and mess charges
  • Grants for purchasing books and essential study materials
  • Special incentives for female students and students from disadvantaged backgrounds

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Education Scholarship Scheme, applicants must meet the following criteria:

Residency

Must be a permanent resident of Andhra Pradesh with valid proof of residence

Educational Qualification

Must have passed Class 10th/12th or equivalent examination from a recognized board

Course Enrollment

Must be enrolled in a full-time undergraduate, postgraduate, or professional course in a recognized institution within Andhra Pradesh

Family Income

Annual family income from all sources must not exceed ₹2,50,000

Academic Performance

Minimum 60% marks or equivalent grade in the last qualifying examination (relaxable for certain categories)

Note: Priority will be given to students from economically weaker sections, SC/ST/OBC categories, and female students. Students pursuing professional courses like Engineering, Medicine, etc., may receive higher scholarship amounts.

Benefits

The Education Scholarship Scheme offers a comprehensive range of benefits to support students in their educational journey:

Tuition Fee Reimbursement

Full or partial reimbursement of tuition fees and other compulsory fees paid to the institution

Book & Stationery Grant

Annual grant for purchasing textbooks, reference books, and essential stationery items

Hostel & Mess Charges

Financial assistance for hostel accommodation and mess expenses for students residing in hostels

Equipment Support

One-time grant for purchasing a laptop or tablet for eligible professional course students

Merit Incentives

Additional incentives for students securing top ranks in university examinations

Coaching Assistance

Support for students preparing for competitive exams (e.g., Civil Services, GATE)

Required Documents

The following documents are required to apply for the Education Scholarship Scheme:

Identity Proof

Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, or any other government-issued ID

Residence Proof

Domicile Certificate, Electricity bill, Water bill, or Ration card

Academic Records

Marksheets of Class 10th, 12th, and previous qualifying examinations

Admission Proof

Bonafide certificate from the institution, Fee receipt of current course

Bank Details

Bank account passbook or cancelled cheque in the student's name

Income Certificate

Family income certificate issued by competent authority (e.g., Tahsildar)

Photographs

Recent passport-sized photographs

Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Caste certificate for SC/ST/OBC applicants

Note: All documents should be self-attested. Original documents may be required for verification during the application process or at the institution.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply if I am studying in a private college?

Yes, students studying in recognized private colleges affiliated with a university in Andhra Pradesh are eligible, provided the college and course are approved for the scholarship.

How is the scholarship amount disbursed?

The scholarship amount is typically disbursed directly to the student's bank account linked with Aadhaar (DBT - Direct Benefit Transfer). In some cases, tuition fees might be paid directly to the institution.

Is there a renewal process for the scholarship?

Yes, most scholarships require annual renewal. Students need to maintain satisfactory academic performance and attendance as per the scheme guidelines to be eligible for renewal.

What if I fail a semester or academic year?

Generally, failing a semester or academic year may make you ineligible for scholarship renewal for that period. Please refer to the specific scheme guidelines for detailed information on academic progress requirements.

Can I avail this scholarship along with other scholarships?

This depends on the terms and conditions of this scholarship and any other scholarship you are receiving. Some schemes may not allow you to avail multiple scholarships simultaneously. Please check the guidelines carefully.
