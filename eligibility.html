Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the eligibility check?

Our eligibility checker uses the official government criteria for each scheme. While we strive for accuracy, the final eligibility determination is made by the concerned government department during the application process.

Is my information secure?

Yes, all your information is secure and encrypted. We do not store any sensitive personal information. Your data is only used to check eligibility and is not shared with any third parties.

What if I'm not eligible for any schemes?

If you're not eligible for any schemes currently, you can check back later as new schemes are added regularly. You can also modify some of your information and check again, as eligibility criteria may change based on your circumstances.

Can I apply for multiple schemes?

Yes, you can apply for multiple schemes as long as you meet the eligibility criteria for each scheme. There are no restrictions on the number of schemes you can apply for.

What documents will I need for the application?

The required documents vary by scheme. When you view the details of a scheme you're eligible for, you'll see a list of required documents. Common documents include identity proof, residence proof, income certificate, and bank details.
