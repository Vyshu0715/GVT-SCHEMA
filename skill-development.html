Overview

The Youth Skill Development Program, prominently known as 'Kaushalya AP', is an initiative by the Andhra Pradesh State Skill Development Corporation (APSSDC) to impart market-driven skills to the youth of the state. The program aims to bridge the gap between industry demands and the existing skill set of the workforce, thereby improving employability and fostering a culture of entrepreneurship.

Kaushalya AP offers a wide array of short-term and long-term courses across various sectors like IT/ITES, manufacturing, construction, healthcare, agriculture, tourism, and more. Training is delivered through a network of government and private training partners, often incorporating industry collaborations for practical exposure and placement assistance.

Key Features of the Scheme

  • Wide range of skill development courses aligned with National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF)
  • Free or highly subsidized training programs
  • Industry-recognized certification upon successful completion
  • Emphasis on practical training and on-the-job experience
  • Placement assistance and job fair participations
  • Support for self-employment and entrepreneurship ventures
  • Special focus on rural youth, women, and disadvantaged groups

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Youth Skill Development Program (Kaushalya AP), applicants generally need to meet these criteria:

Age Group

Typically between 18 to 35 years (may vary for specific courses or target groups)

Residency

Must be a resident of Andhra Pradesh

Educational Qualification

Varies by course (e.g., 8th pass, 10th pass, 12th pass, ITI, Diploma, Graduates). Some courses may not have strict educational prerequisites.

Employment Status

Primarily for unemployed youth, but some upskilling courses may be available for employed individuals.

Note: Specific courses may have additional eligibility criteria related to aptitude or prior knowledge. Relaxation in age or qualification may be available for certain categories as per government norms.

Benefits

The Youth Skill Development Program offers numerous benefits to empower the youth:

Quality Skill Training

Access to industry-relevant training programs delivered by qualified trainers and institutions

Certification

Government-recognized (NSQF aligned) certification upon successful course completion, enhancing job prospects

Placement Assistance

Support in finding employment through job fairs, campus placements, and industry linkages

Entrepreneurship Support

Guidance and linkages for those interested in starting their own ventures post-training

Learning Material

Access to course materials, and sometimes toolkits or uniforms depending on the trade

Stipend / Financial Support

Some courses may offer a stipend or support for travel and accommodation, especially for residential programs

Required Documents

The following documents are generally required for enrolling in skill development courses:

Identity Proof

Aadhaar Card (mandatory)

Residence Proof

Aadhaar Card, Ration Card, or Domicile Certificate

Educational Certificates

Marksheets and certificates of qualifying examinations (as per course requirement)

Photographs

Recent passport-sized photographs

Caste Certificate

If applying under reserved categories (SC/ST/OBC)

Bank Account Details

Passbook copy for stipend transfer, if applicable

Note: Specific courses might require additional documents. It's best to check the course brochure or contact the training center.

Application Process

Enrolling in a skill development course under Kaushalya AP involves these general steps:

1

Course Identification

Explore available courses on the APSSDC portal or SmartSahyata based on interest and eligibility

2

Online Registration

3

Counseling & Selection

4

Document Verification

Complete enrollment formalities and commence the training program

6

Assessment & Certification

7

Avail placement assistance provided by the training center and APSSDC

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the training programs completely free?

Most courses offered under Kaushalya AP are either completely free or highly subsidized. Some specialized courses might have a nominal fee. This information is provided in the course details.

What is the duration of these skill development courses?

The duration varies widely depending on the course and skill level, ranging from a few weeks (e.g., 200 hours) to several months (e.g., 600-1200 hours).

Is there any guarantee of a job after completing the course?

While there is no absolute guarantee, APSSDC and its training partners make significant efforts to provide placement assistance through job fairs and industry connections. The employability of a candidate also depends on their performance and skills acquired.

Can I enroll in multiple courses at the same time?

Generally, it is advisable to enroll in and complete one course at a time to focus effectively. Some short, complementary courses might be an exception, but this needs to be checked with the training provider.

Where are the training centers located?

Training centers are located across all districts of Andhra Pradesh. The APSSDC portal provides a list of courses and their respective training center locations. Some courses may also be offered online.
