Frequently Asked Questions

How can I check the status of my inquiry?

When you submit an inquiry through our contact form, you'll receive a reference number. You can use this reference number to check the status of your inquiry by calling our helpline or visiting any of our help centers.

What is the typical response time for inquiries?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during working days. For complex inquiries that require coordination with specific government departments, it may take 3-5 working days.

Can I visit the help center without an appointment?

Yes, you can visit any of our help centers during working hours without an appointment. However, if you'd like to ensure minimal waiting time, we recommend scheduling an appointment by calling our helpline.

What documents should I bring when visiting a help center?

When visiting a help center, please bring a valid ID proof (Aadhaar card, voter ID, etc.), any relevant application IDs or reference numbers, and copies of documents related to your inquiry or application.
